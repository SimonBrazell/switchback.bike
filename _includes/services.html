Sometimes your bike may need new components or accessories installed. I will contact you before any additional items are fitted before proceeding with the work. Most of the time these items can be installed at no extra labour charge if you opt for a service.

If you are looking for something specific to be done to your bike, please call or email to discuss your needs. Most bicycle related work is possible. Some examples of specific work include:

  • Tubeless tyre setup & inflation
  • Cable fitting & adjustment
  • Tubular tyre fitting
  • Electronic gearing adjustment
  • Spoke replacement
  • Derailleur hanger alignment
  • Custom wheel building
  • Hydraulic brake bleeding & servicing
  • Frame alignment checks
  • Disc brake decontamination
  • Bottom bracket tapping & facing
  • Custom bike builds
